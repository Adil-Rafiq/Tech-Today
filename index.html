IN COLLABORATION WITH WIE LAHORE SECTION

About Tech Today

WIE Lahore Section in collaboration with WIE LUMS conducted this event, technical talk & women empowerment session at LUMS on January 06, 2020. IEEE LUMS Student Chapter and WIE LUMS conducts this workshop every year to motivate and empower women working in technology. This year our talks has Soch se agey, empower women to empower economy(an initiative of Punjab govt., Technical Talk (Azure, Conversational AI) and Machine Learning: Python for Data Preprocessing & Visualisation (Hands-on Training).

Event Proceedings

Talk 1

Jowaria and Safoora first took the stage to talk on the topic "Soch se agay". They mainly talked about that how women can achieve their goals. They talked about their journey and how they turned their dreams into reality. Audience was particularly inspired by their story of how they became what they are today. After all, it all comes down to persistence, determination and out of the box thinking that helps anyone achieve their goals. They also talked about opportunities for women especially related to WIE and IEEE etc.

Talk 2

Saad Mahmood's took the stage to talk about technical topics including Azure and conversational AI. He started off with a gentle introduction to azure and conversational AI, career opportunities in the field, the role of in our daily life etc. And built his way to the hands-on workshop which gave a more practical and technical approach to the audience.

Talk 3

Barrister Malik Salman Aslam focused on "Empower women to empower economy". As evident from the topic, he insisted on women empowerment and talked about the approaches that would help empowering women. He also talked about current government plans, the opportunities that women have at the moment and the hurdles that we still need to overcome in order to empower women. He also discussed how's there's been a wave of huge support to strengthen women through supporting their entrepreneurial ideas and business plans and how it's helping shaping and building the current economy.

Talk 4

Mubashra Manzoor's talked was focused on Machine learning. She started off with a gentle introduction to Machine Learning, career opportunities in the field, the role of Machine learning in our daily life etc. And built her way to the hands-on workshop which gave a more practical approach to the audience.
